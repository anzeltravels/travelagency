CITY OF JOY-KOLKATA

Kolkata the capital of West Bengal, India is a charming city with great culture, heritage and tradition. Famous for its Durga Puja, Kolkata is enriched with colorful lights and vibrant festivals with river Hooghly winding along. Kolkata is the only city in India to still retain trams that bring back memories of historic days.It is the third most populous city in India after Mumbai and Delhi and third for its GDP. Its the cultural, commercial and educational hub of Eastern India. The place is famous for art, music, literature and sports.
